⚙️ How It Works

Step-by-step process explanation

01
📹
Video Upload / Camera
User Streamlit app mein traffic video upload karta hai ya live camera connect karta hai.
02
⚡
Frame-by-Frame Detection
YOLOv8s model har frame mein vehicles ko bounding box ke saath detect karta hai — ~28 FPS.
03
🧮
PCU Score Calculate
Detected vehicles ka PCU weight multiply karke total traffic load calculate hota hai.
04
📈
Time-Series Build
Last 60 frames ka PCU data collect hota hai LSTM ke liye input sequence banana ke liye.
05
🔮
BiLSTM Prediction
Bidirectional LSTM model agle 10 minute ka traffic load predict karta hai with high accuracy.
06
🚨
Congestion Alert
Low / Moderate / High alert system traffic authorities ko advance warning deta hai.
